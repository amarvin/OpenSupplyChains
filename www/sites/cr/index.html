“Cotton Road” travels from family farms in South Carolina to the factories of China to tell a story about globalized labor, weaving a portrait of the work and workers we never see and the products that they make. Cotton Road introduces consumers to the people and industrial processes behind the transnational cotton trade. Who are the workers that make your clothing? And how are the things you consume actually produced?
Directed and Produced by Laura Kissel with Co Directing and Production in China by Li Zhen 李桢
