Cotton Road 棉花之路

“Cotton Road” travels from family farms in South Carolina to the factories of China to tell a story about globalized labor, weaving a portrait of the work and workers we never see and the products that they make.
  • Movie Trailer
  • Sourcemap
Track the travels of cotton from an agricultural field in South Carolina to factories in China and witness the manufacturing of products we desire to consume. Connecting our consumption to the labor behind it, “Cotton Road” presents a portrait of globalized work in the 21st century.
Cotton Road introduces consumers to the people and industrial processes behind the transnational cotton trade. Who are the workers that make your clothing? And how are the things you consume actually produced?
